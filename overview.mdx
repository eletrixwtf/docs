## About Untitled Game

_Untitled Game_ is an in-development Roblox roleplay experience inspired by _Emergency Response: Liberty County_ (ERLC), aiming to deliver an immersive open-world environment with roles like police, firefighters, paramedics, and civilians along with roleplaying abilities.
