## About Untitled Game

_Untitled Game_ is an in-development Roblox roleplay experience inspired by _Emergency Response: Liberty County_ (ERLC), aiming to deliver an immersive open-world environment with roles like police, firefighters, paramedics, and civilians along with roleplaying abilities.

## Current Scope

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Core Mechanics Development" icon="palette">

  </Card>
  <Card title="World Design" icon="square-code">
    Making an open-world map with a properly finished city with key locations such as police stations, hospitals etc.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
